About

Coding done right!

After creating and refining multiple webpages for his favorite game Starcraft during his teenage years, Eugene realized he had a natural talent for designing and creating webpages. After years of following other passions and learning about himself Eugene returned to web design to follow his true passion of sparking peoples intrests and intimately connecting with them.

Coding that connects!

Life is about connection and Eugene excels in creating dynamic, responsive websites that connect clients with the wants and needs of their customer base. Through his use of Wordsmithery, HTML5, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, AJAX, and PHP Eugene can make your design wants and needs a reality. Eugene believes that anything worth doing is worth doing right and that designs should be effective, innovative, and sleek.
